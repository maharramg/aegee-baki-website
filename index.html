Who we are

AEGEE is one of Europe’s biggest interdisciplinary student organizations. As a non-governmental, politically independent, and non-profit organization AEGEE ( www.aegee.org) is open to students and young people from all faculties and disciplines. Founded in 1985 in Paris, today AEGEE has grown to a Network of 13000 AEGEEans, present in 161 cities in 40 countries all over Europe. AEGEE-Bakı is the main representative of AEGEE-Europe in Baku, Azerbaijan. The main goal of organization is to achieve the development of tolerant and open societies by carrying out the cultural exchange among the youth in Europe.
